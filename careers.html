Junior Records Revision Specialist

Full-Time · All Sectors · Immediate Start

Overview

Assist senior specialists in the daily correction and alignment of historical records. You will work directly with archival materials, applying the Ministry's five-step correction protocol to ensure all publicly accessible information reflects current approved standards.

Responsibilities

  • Process and correct assigned archival records according to daily quotas
  • Cross-reference corrections against the Central Approved Narrative Database
  • Flag complex inconsistencies for senior review
  • Maintain detailed correction logs for compliance auditing
  • Participate in weekly alignment briefings

Requirements

  • Minimum 2 years of approved education
  • Demonstrated loyalty assessment score of 85% or above
  • No prior discrepancy flags or compliance violations
  • Proficiency in Newspeak (B1 level minimum)
  • Willingness to work extended hours during correction surges

Compensation

Standard Ministry salary band (Grade 3), Ministry housing allocation, nutrition program enrollment, and 15 approved recreation days per year. Performance bonuses awarded for exceeding correction quotas.
