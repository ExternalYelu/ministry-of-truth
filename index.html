Our Mission

Clarity. Unity. Consistency.

The Ministry of Truth is dedicated to the preservation of informational integrity across all sectors of Oceania. Through diligent oversight and continuous improvement, we ensure that every citizen has access to the most current and accurate version of reality.

Stability

A stable society requires stable information. We eliminate contradictions before they create confusion.

Stability ensures productivity. Inconsistency is the root of doubt, and doubt is the root of disorder.

Accuracy

Accuracy is not a fixed state — it is a process of continuous refinement toward optimal alignment.

What was true yesterday may require adjustment today. This is not change — it is improvement.

Unity

When all citizens share the same understanding, society moves forward as one.

Unity is the natural result of alignment. Divergent perspectives are addressed through guidance, not punishment.
